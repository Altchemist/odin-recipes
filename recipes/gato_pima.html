Gato Pima So So !

Ingredients

  • 100g yellow split peas
  • 2 Spring Onions
  • 2 Chilli - dried red
  • 2 Chilli - fresh green birds eye
  • 1 Tbs Salt
  • 1/2 bunch Coriander including the stalk

Please note that the yellow split peas need to be pre-soaked in water for 4-6 hours before preparation

Steps

  1. In a food processor grind the yellow split peas until it resembles a fine paste, this takes around 5 minutes. Remove from food processor and place into a large mixing bowl.
  2. Finely chop spring onions, chilli and coriander, and crush in the dried chilli and add this to the yellow spit peas. Mix thoroughly and season with salt.
  3. sing a deep fat fryer set the temperature to 180, roll the mixture into small balls and deep fry until golden brown.

How to enjoy it ?

Pick up a loaf of bread, a salted butter pack and a hot cup of tea. Sit down, spread some butter on the bread, put some "gato pima" on the bread and cherish it

P.S. For truly the best experience, a rainy, cold or misty weather can really add to the mood
